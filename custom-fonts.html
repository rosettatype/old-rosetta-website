Custom branding tool

Custom fonts infuse your brand and reinforce authenticity in every message you write. With our proven strengths in blending distinctive design with performance, let us design brand new fonts exclusively for you, or tailor some to fit your specific needs from the range of our retail fonts.

Design for a wide palette of world languages

Rosetta specialises in designing for numerous languages and writing systems (scripts). Be it a single Central European language or a set of complex Asian scripts, we know how to design for them, and our assurance of quality extends to all the scripts we design.

Smart fonts = better design

Custom fonts can improve on-screen readability, print clarity and space efficiency. They address a wide range of todays design problems. When made smart, they are able to streamline complex typesetting requirements such as tables, formulas or graphs.
